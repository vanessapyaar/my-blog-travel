I have been working with web development since 2018 at the same company. Covid ended up interrupting activities at the office, and now my office is my computer, wherever I am. :)

I work with web development and I have been with the same company since 2018, which headquarters are located in Miami. With covid, the office is closed and we are all working from our computers, until God knows when.

Despite the regrets, we managed to find a Cowork in the city and during working hours we are there, beating the clock, holding meetings and waiting for the "expedient" to end so we can explore the city.

Freedom has been a delight, knowing that we are not on vacation, but that we have the opportunity to visit new places and experience a different culture is rewarding! A rare opportunity that I knew I should take, last as long as it lasts!
