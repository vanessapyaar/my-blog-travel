Cartagena de Indias, Colombia

November 8th, 2020 • by Vanessa

We reached the end of our first week working remotely in Colombia!

Mario and I both work in the Tech field, 100% remote and are based in Florida, the sunshine state. With COVID and everything that happened, we decideed to pack our bags and go for a not-so-short voyage through the Paisa Country.

We didn't have many plans around the itinerary but we both know we wanted to get to know the most we could in about 3 to 4 months - while working full-time!

I am not so sure why we choose Colombia to be the guinea-pig of our working/traveling-full-time experiment. We will discover that soon enough!

We landed in Cargatena and decided to stay in a cozy AirBnb inside the walled city. So far, so good!
